Crosshairs Audit Lab/Inductive Symmetry Audit/Theory Notes

Model source

Inductive symmetry, not mere disagreement.

The audit is grounded in the idea that relevantly similar inductive assessments should be governed by the same evidential standards unless a real differentiator is named and justified.

Read the grounding paper

Inductive Symmetry

If an argument treats one observed pattern as forceful, similar patterns should receive comparable permission unless relevant differences are identified.

Modal Smuggling

Modal smuggling happens when an observed regularity is quietly promoted into necessity without an independent bridge premise.

Scope Drift

Scope drift occurs when evidence gathered inside one domain is used to support a claim in another domain without showing that the transfer is licensed.

Specificity Inflation

Specificity inflation occurs when modest evidence for a broad claim is treated as evidence for a much richer doctrinal claim.

How the app uses the model

The tool asks users to expose their anchor induction, compare it with parallel inductions, classify any differentiators, and inspect the remaining tension. The score is a diagnostic pressure reading, not a proof that a conclusion is false.
