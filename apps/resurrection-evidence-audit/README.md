# Resurrection Evidence Audit

Static Crosshairs audit for resurrection, miracle, and answered-prayer claims.

The app focuses on:

- Explicit prior decomposition: general divine action, targeting specificity, and act-type rate.
- Evidence ledger: `P(E|claim)`, `P(E|not claim)`, and independence weight per evidence item.
- Required Bayes factors for 50%, 90%, and 99% confidence.
- Pitfall flags for prior stacking, suppressed alternatives, dependence inflation, testimony-to-truth transfer, neutral evidence, and missing unknown reserves.
- Postdiction comparator for grief, dissonance, scriptural rereading, memory, source dependence, and communal reinforcement.

Source basis:

- `Abduction_vs_Bayes_Competing_Frameworks.pdf`
- `Bayesian_Pitfalls_in_Resurrection_Apolo.pdf`
- `Emotional_Drivers_and_Resurrection_Clai.pdf`
- Free of Faith posts on resurrection calculus, Bayesian variables, expectations, and resurrection plausibility.
